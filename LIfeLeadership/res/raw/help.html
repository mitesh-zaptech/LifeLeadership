Help

Welcome to the Rascal Radio Help page, the place to come to become an expert in Rascal Radio-ology! Rascal Radio gives you complete control of what you want to listen to. It allows to you to create and save your own stations based on speaker and subject matter or simply play the radio in shuffle mode, which randomly plays audios from the entire database of life-changing information. Below are a few helpful hints.

How to Create a Station

  • If you’d like to create a particular speaker or subject station, press the Select a Speaker or Select a Subject button, select the desired speaker or subject, close the menu, then press Play. If you wish to play selections for both a particular speaker and a particular subject, make selections from both the Speaker and Subject menus individually before pressing Play.

How to Save a Station

  • To save a Station, simply press the My Stations icon while listening to the Station. The My Stations icon is the Heart shaped icon and will contain a "+" if the Station is not currently saved. After saving a Station, the Station will show up in the menu under My Stations. You can also remove a saved Station when listening to that particular Station by pressing the My Stations icon again (which will display a heart with a "-").

How to Play/Pause Rascal Radio

  • The Play button is the large grey button with the red arrow. Simply press Play to start playing the Rascal Radio station of your choice. After you start playing the radio, the Play button then becomes a Pause button, which has two vertical grey lines on it. Hit the button to pause the radio.

  • If you’d like to create a particular speaker or subject station, press the Select a Speaker or Select a Subject button, select the desired speaker or subject, close the menu, then press Play. If you wish to play selections for both a particular speaker and a particular subject, make selections from both the Speaker and Subject menus individually before pressing Play.

  • If you wish to play all your Liked Audios press the menu button in the upper left. Then choose Liked Audios and the Play.

  • If you wish to play your My Stations, go to the menu in the upper left. You can view all saved stations. Choose the station you wish to play. Rascal Radio will then play all selections for that station, either the Speaker, Subject or the Speaker and Subject combination you have saved.

  • If you aren't exactly sure what you want to listen to, press the menu button in the upper left. Then choose Shuffle and then Play. This will play a random selection of all speakers and subjects.

Operational Icons

  • Like (thumbs up) - Adds that specific selection to your Liked Audios list for easy access later on. You can also Unlike a Liked talk if you wish to remove it from the list at a later time.

  • Skip - Press the Skip icon if you wish to Skip a selection. Rascal Radio allows 5 Skips per hour. The number of Skips you have remaining will be displayed on the Skip Icon.

  • My Stations (heart with + or -) - Adds or removes the currently viewed station (either the Speaker or Subject or combination) to/from your My Stations list in the menu. The currently viewed station will be displayed in the white text box above the Play button. The Heart with the "+" adds the station to My Stations. The Heart with the "-" removes a station from My Stations.

Rascal Radio Menu Options

  • My Stations - View/play your preferred Speaker and/or Subject at a quick glance.
  • Shuffle - Tell Rascal Radio to enter Shuffle mode.
  • Liked Audios - Tell Rascal Radio to play all Liked Audios.
  • Help - Well…you figured that one out.

  • Privacy - View Rascal Radio Privacy Policy.
  • Contact - Send and email to LIFE Support.
  • Logout - Logout of Rascal Radio.
